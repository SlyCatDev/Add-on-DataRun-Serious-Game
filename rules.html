How to play

Objective

Become the Master of the Web by being the first player to reach the final space on the game board!

Setup

  1. Unfold the game board and place it on a flat surface.
  2. Each player selects a pawn and places it on the starting space.
  3. Shuffle the cards from the categories: Social Media, Cybersecurity, Geek Culture, and Internet History into three difficulty levels (Easy, Medium, Hard), and organize them into four separate piles.
  4. Decide the turn order by rolling the die; the highest number goes first.

Gameplay

During each turn, players perform the following actions:

  • Roll the Die: Move your pawn forward according to the number rolled.
  • Resolve the Space: Follow the instructions of the space landed on:
    • Category Space: Answer a question. Correct answers move you forward (1 to 3 spaces depending on difficulty); wrong answers move you back.
    • Special Spaces:
      • Virus: Return to the starting space.
      • Crypto Bonus: Move forward 3 spaces immediately.
      • Unstable Wi-Fi: Lose a turn.
      • Server Crash: Move back 2 spaces.
      • Pirate: Swap positions with a player of your choice.

Winning Conditions

The first player to reach the final space becomes the Master of the Web. If multiple players reach the final space simultaneously, they must answer a final Hard question to determine the winner.
